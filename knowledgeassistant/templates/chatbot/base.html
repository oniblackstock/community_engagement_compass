How it works
🧠 What Powers This System?

Our system uses a lean, efficient AI model built specifically for community engagement. It’s not a massive, energy-heavy LLM — instead, it’s fast, focused, and low-impact.

  • ⚡ Fast, task-specific performance
  • 🌱 Low energy usage = eco-friendly
  • 🔍 Transparent and accountable architecture
🔎 Transparency & Sustainability

We care about being responsible with technology. This model was built with sustainability in mind:

  • 💡 No unnecessary energy consumption
  • 📉 Minimal compute overhead
  • 📖 View model documentation to learn more

About Community Engagement Compass

The Community Engagement Compass is designed to help practitioners explore trusted, curated content with confidence. Ask questions directly against your document set and receive clear, cited answers — every time.

  • 📄 Uploaded documents are securely processed and organized for efficient search and response.
  • 🔗 Responses include citations that link directly to the source page for full transparency.
  • 🔐 Your organization’s content is kept private and scoped exclusively to your account.
